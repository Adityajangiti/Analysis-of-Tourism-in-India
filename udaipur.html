UDAIPUR

Udaipur, also known as the "City of Lakes" is a beautiful city located in the Indian state of Rajasthan. It is known for its stunning palaces, serene lakes, and rich cultural heritage. Here are some of the top places to visit in Udaipur:

  • City Palace: The City Palace is a magnificent complex of palaces, courtyards, and gardens located on the banks of Lake Pichola. It was built over a period of 400 years and is a great example of Rajasthani and Mughal architecture.
  • Lake Pichola: Lake Pichola is a picturesque lake located in the heart of Udaipur. It is surrounded by hills, palaces, temples, and ghats, making it a popular spot for boat rides and evening walks.
  • Jag Mandir: Jag Mandir is a beautiful palace located on an island in Lake Pichola. It was built in the 17th century and is known for its exquisite architecture and stunning views of the lake.
  • Saheliyon-ki-Bari: Saheliyon-ki-Bari is a beautiful garden located on the banks of Fateh Sagar Lake. It was built in the 18th century for the women of the royal household and is known for its fountains, lotus pools, and marble elephants.
  • Fateh Sagar Lake: Fateh Sagar Lake is a serene lake located in the north of Udaipur. It is surrounded by hills and offers stunning views of the city and its surroundings.
  • Monsoon Palace: The Monsoon Palace is a hilltop palace located on the outskirts of Udaipur. It was built in the 19th century and is known for its stunning views of the city and its surroundings.
  • Jagdish Temple: Jagdish Temple is a beautiful temple located in the heart of Udaipur. It was built in the 17th century and is known for its exquisite architecture and intricate carvings.
  • Vintage Car Museum: The Vintage Car Museum is a unique museum located in Udaipur. It houses a collection of vintage cars, including Rolls Royce, Mercedes-Benz, and Cadillac.
  • Shilpgram: Shilpgram is a cultural village located on the outskirts of Udaipur. It showcases the art and craft of Rajasthan and is a great place to buy souvenirs.
  • Badi Lake: Badi Lake is a beautiful lake located in the outskirts of Udaipur. It is surrounded by hills and is a popular spot for picnics and boating.
