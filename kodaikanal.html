KODAIKANAL

Kodaikanal is a beautiful hill station located in the southern Indian state of Tamil Nadu. Here are some of the top places to visit in Kodaikanal:

  • Kodaikanal Lake - A picturesque lake located in the heart of Kodaikanal, it is a popular spot for boating and cycling. Surrounded by lush green hills, the lake is a great place to relax and enjoy the scenic beauty of the region.
  • Coaker's Walk - A scenic walking trail located on the edge of a steep slope, Coaker's Walk offers panoramic views of the valley below and the surrounding mountains. It is a great place to watch the sunrise and sunset.
  • Bryant Park - A beautiful botanical garden located near Kodaikanal Lake, Bryant Park is known for its colorful flower beds, exotic plants, and a variety of trees. It is a popular spot for picnics and nature walks.
  • Pillar Rocks - A set of three giant rock pillars standing tall at a height of 400 feet, Pillar Rocks offers stunning views of the surrounding valleys and the mountains. It is a popular spot for trekking and rock climbing.
  • Bear Shola Falls - A beautiful waterfall located in the dense forest area, Bear Shola Falls is a great place to relax and enjoy the natural beauty of Kodaikanal. The waterfall is surrounded by lush greenery and is a popular spot for bird watching.
  • Dolphin's Nose - A scenic viewpoint located on the edge of a cliff, Dolphin's Nose offers breathtaking views of the surrounding valleys and the mountains. It is a great place to enjoy the panoramic views of the region.
  • Kurinji Andavar Temple - A popular temple dedicated to Lord Murugan, the Kurinji Andavar Temple is known for its beautiful architecture and scenic location. The temple is surrounded by lush green hills and offers stunning views of the valley below.

    • Luggage to carry

    When packing for a tour of Kodaikanal, it's important to keep in mind the climate and activities you'll be doing. Here are some suggested items to include in your luggage:

  • Warm clothes - Kodaikanal can get chilly, especially at night, so be sure to pack warm clothes like sweaters, jackets, and socks.
  • Comfortable walking shoes - You'll likely be doing a lot of walking, so be sure to pack comfortable shoes that are suitable for trekking or hiking.
  • Sunscreen and sunglasses - While Kodaikanal's climate is generally cool and pleasant, the sun can still be strong during the day, so it's important to protect your skin and eyes.
  • Umbrella or raincoat - Kodaikanal receives moderate rainfall, so it's a good idea to carry an umbrella or raincoat.
  • Camera - Kodaikanal is a beautiful place, and you'll want to capture all the stunning scenery and picturesque landscapes on your camera.
  • Medications and first aid kit - It's important to pack any necessary medications and a small first aid kit for emergencies.
  • Backpack or daypack - A backpack or daypack is handy for carrying essentials like water bottles, snacks, and extra layers of clothing while exploring Kodaikanal.
  • Remember to pack light and only bring what you'll need for your trip to avoid overpacking and unnecessary luggage weight.
